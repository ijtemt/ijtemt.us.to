IJTEMT at a glance

As the publisher in the field of Economics Management and Technology, IJTEMT serves many Researchers, Students, Academecians & Professionals Worldwide.

We help new researchers by providing world-class information and research work that helps them make critical decisions, enhance productivity and improve outcomes.

  • Mission:
    Mission of IJTEMT is to foster a inter disciplinary research in the field of Economics Management and Technology, to bridge the gap between academicians, researchers and practitioners to foster better understanding and results for enhancing fruitful outcome, hence helping mankind.
  • Who we are:
    IJTEMT is a bi-monthly double-blind peer reviewed refereed open access International e-Journal, providing instant worldwide and barrier-free access to the full-text of all published manuscripts to all interested readers in the best interest of the research community. Publication of manuscripts in IJTEMT Journal is a direct reflection of quality research work done by the authors. The prevention of publication malpractices is one of the important responsibilities of our editorial board. It is expected from every author that they will submit accurate information and sufficient details relevant to their research paper. Any kind of unethical behaviour is not acceptable. IJTEMT journal does not tolerate any kind of plagiarism and is committed for upholding the highest standards of publication ethics and takes all possible measures against any publication malpractices. Authors submitting articles to IJTEMT Journal, affirm that the contents of their manuscript are original. Furthermore, they have to sign a copyright transfer form and undertaking that, their article has neither been published elsewhere in any language fully or partly, nor is it under review for publication anywhere. In case of any dispute, the decision of editorial board will prevail.

  • History:
    International Journal of Trends in Economics Management and Technology (IJTEMT) is an International Academic Bi-Monthly Journal e-published in The United States of America and open to the world. In this present interdisciplinary era, here at IJTEMT, a group of intellectuals came together to find a common platform for three major components in any economy viz. Economics, Management and Technology. Here we provide a forum to bridge the gap between the brushed-up professionals in their respective fields and the new researcher which will result in better understanding and fruitful outcomes. Since then we have published various research papers in the field of Economics, Management and Technology & still counting. more
  • Services:
    A good Quality Online and Offline Bi-monthly Publication of IJTEMT. We organise guest lecture for various universities, institutes, colleges, departments and industries by and for the renowned academicians, scientists and researchers, internationally.
