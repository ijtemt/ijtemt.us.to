RESEARCH EXPERIENCE (DISSERTATION & PROJECTS)

i. Doctoral dissertation:
College of Arts and Sciences, Economics School, Universiti Utara Malaysia, Sintok, Kedah. Supervisor: Professor Mohd. Zaini Abdul Karim.
  a Title: Banking efficiency in developing countries.
  b Measure the cost and profit efficiency of the commercial banks in the selected developing countries in Asia, Middle East and North Africa, and Africa region.
  c Determine the bank-specific factors and macroeconomics variables that influence the bank efficiency of the commercial banks for each region.
  d Method used in analysis: Standard translog specification, Fourier Flexible functional form, Data Envelopment Analysis, and Tobit regression.
ii. Master dissertation:
Faculty of Economics, Universiti Utara Malaysia, Sintok, Kedah. Supervisor: Associate Professor Mohd. Zaini Abdul Karim.
  a Title: Integration Analysis of the Malaysian Stock Market
  b Analyse cointegration and causal relationship between the Malaysian stock market performances with its major trading partners.
  c Method used in analysis: Johansen cointegration and Vector Error- correction model.
