AYUSH GUPTA

Curriculum Vitae

Corporate Trainer and Training Coordinator at GAIL India Ltd,
Noida, Uttar Pradesh, India

 

AYUSH GUPTA is an astute professional with over 21 years of experience in Process and Oil & Gas Industry, encompassing Project Management, Operations and Maintenance, Quality, MIS and Training. After working for over 10 years at UP Region O&M Headquarters, Agra looking after the Project Construction, Operations & Maintenance of Cross country Gas Pipelines and Agra & Firozabad City Gas Distribution Network, I am presently working as Chief Manager(Training) at GAIL India Ltd and posted at GAIL Training Institute, Noida.  The current job profile includes working as a faculty for design, development, and delivery of courses related to Natural Gas Pipeline Projects Management, O&M, Cathodic Protection and skill development. The responsibility includes planning and execution of learning & development interventions for Human resource development & capability building at GAIL. Having experience in plant operations and maintenance including conceptualization, planning, and execution of various related activities. Possess technical expertise in Project Management, Construction and O&M of natural gas pipelines including compliance to various codes and standards (ASME, API, NACE, ISO, OISD etc.) during construction, commissioning and O&M of natural gas pipelines.

Core Areas of Expertise: City Gas Distribution Project Management and O&M, Natural Gas Pipelines Projects and O&M, Cathodic Protection, Integrity Management, Human Resources Capability Building, Talent Management

Operations and Maintenance

  • Maintaining uninterrupted gas supplies to over 300 consumers in Agra and Firozabad region.
  • Monitoring & maintenance of 450 kms Natural Gas pipeline network including over 100 Kms of Agra & Firozabad City Gas Distribution Pipeline Network and their associated Pipeline installations (SV, IP & RR Station, City Gate Station).
  • Monitoring and maintenance of Electrical System (HT/LT System, GEG, TEG & Other Electrical Equipment).
  • Preventive and Breakdown maintenance including planning and effecting preventive maintenance schedules for various machineries and equipment in an Indo-German Silica Manufacturing Process Plant.
  • Spares procurement along with finalizing the specifications of spare parts and establishing the quality & quantity limits.
  • Implementation of PM module of SAP-ERP for O&M at work centres of GAIL UP Region.
  • Tendering and execution of contracts for maintenance gas pipelines and Cathodic Protection system.
  • Effectively handling the emergency situations like leakage and fire in the city gas distribution pipelines.
  • Implementing innovative measures to prevent meter tampering and theft of gas.
  • Administration of Gas supply contracts for over 300 small scale industrial customers including ensuring supply of gas as per contract, proper metering, invoicing and ensuring compliance to the given terms and conditions.
  • Liaisoning with the District administration, Other Utility companies, local MPs/MLAs, Industry associations etc.
  • Implementation of the first pilot system for remote monitoring and recording of metering data at the consumer premises.

 

 

Project Planning & Management in City Gas Distribution Networks and Cross Country Natural Gas Pipelines

  • Technical feasibility studies for new consumer connections and pipelines in City area.
  • Detailed engineering and working with engineering consultants like EIL and MECON for expansion of pipeline network, adding new connections and modifications in the existing pipeline networks.
  • Forming and spearheading cross-functional teams for streamlining the execution of project.
  • Regular monitoring and progress review, ensuring timely execution and cost control. 
  • Managing and controlling the resources for optimum utilisation.
  • Generating various reports on a daily, weekly and monthly basis to monitor the progress and functioning as well as apprising the top management of the same.
  • Coordination between the Project Management Consultants(PMC) and the Contractors.

 

Project Execution- Installation and Commissioning in City Gas Distribution Networks and Cross Country Natural Gas Pipelines

  • Managing overall activities pertaining to installation and commissioning of GREP Pipeline, Bajhera-Agra-Firozabad Spurline, and Agra & Firozabad city gas Pipelines and their associated Cathodic Protection systems.
  • Supervising commissioning activities and ensuring adherence to various codes and standards as well as compliance to quality and safety standards.
  • Conducting inspection, pre-commissioning and functional testing of various equipment and systems.
  • Installation and commissioning of Thermo Electric Generators that were being used for the first time in India.
