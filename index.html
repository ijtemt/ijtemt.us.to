International Journal of Trends in Economics Management and Technology (IJTEMT) is an International Academic bi-monthly Journal e-published in UNITED STATES and open to the world. In this present interdisciplinary era, here at IJTEMT, a group of intellectuals came together to find a common platform for three major components in any economy viz. Economics, Management and Technology. Here we provide a forum to bridge the gap between the brushed-up professionals in their respective fields and the new researcher which will result in better understanding and fruitful outcomes. The focus of this journal is to publish papers on Economics, Management and Technology. The submitted papers are reviewed in a full double – blind manner by respective technical committee(s) of the journal. The audience for the journal are professionals from related fields, academicians, new students & research scholars. All submitted articles should report original, previously unpublished research results either experimental or theoretical. All articles will be peer-reviewed. Articles submitted to the journal should meet these criteria and must not be under consideration for publication elsewhere. Manuscripts should follow the style of the journal and will be subject to both, reviewing and editing.

  • Frequency: 6 issues per year (bi-monthly)
